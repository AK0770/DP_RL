üéØ The Mission: End the Guessing Game

Setting the right price is tough. Too high, and your products sit on the shelf. Too low, and you're leaving money on the table. For decades, businesses have relied on static prices or simple rules, which is like navigating a maze blindfolded.

Our mission was to build an AI that could see the maze from above. We wanted an intelligent agent that could dynamically adjust prices based on real-time market signals‚Äîlike inventory levels and customer demand‚Äîto maximize profit, not just sales.

üß† The Game Plan: Teach an AI to Win

We turned this business problem into a game. The AI's goal? To get the highest score possible. The score, in this case, was the **total profit after a 30-day sales period**.

This approach, called **Reinforcement Learning**, is powerful because the AI learns from experience. We didn't give it any rules or hints. We just let it play the "pricing game" over and over, rewarding it only for the final profit. This forced it to discover complex, long-term strategies that a human might never find. It learned that sometimes, a patient, high-price strategy is far more profitable than selling out on day one.

The Arena (pricing_env.py)

A custom Python simulation that acts as the training ground. It's a mini-universe with its own economy, customers, and inventory system where our AI could practice without any real-world risk.

The Champion (The PPO Model)

The "brain" of our operation is the Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) algorithm. Implemented using the robust Stable-Baselines3 library, this is the state-of-the-art technology that powers our agent's decision-making.

The Training Montage (train_model.py)

This is where the magic happens. We subjected our AI to an intense training regimen of 500,000 simulations. With each round, it got a little smarter, refining its strategy until it became a master of pricing. The final, elite model was saved as best_model.zip.

The Mission Control (The Dashboard)

The FastAPI backend and Streamlit frontend work together to create an interactive dashboard. This is the bridge between the AI's complex brain and the user, providing instant, intelligent pricing recommendations.
